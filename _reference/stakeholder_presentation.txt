SLIDE 10: PROPOSED SOLUTION - MULTI-LAYER DETECTION
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

STRATEGY: Defense in Depth
───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

    Layer 1: DEVICE INTEGRITY CHECK
    ├── Knox status (Samsung): Cannot be faked
    ├── Bootloader status: Hard to fake
    └── Effectiveness: 90-95%

    Layer 2: ENVIRONMENTAL VERIFICATION
    ├── WiFi BSSID check (office network)
    ├── Cell tower ID verification
    └── Effectiveness: 85%

    Layer 3: BEHAVIORAL ANALYSIS
    ├── GPS accuracy pattern (too perfect = suspicious)
    ├── Movement analysis (teleport impossible)
    └── Effectiveness: 70%

    Layer 4: PHYSICAL VERIFICATION
    ├── Photo dengan timestamp (live, not gallery)
    ├── Biometric optional
    └── Effectiveness: 90%

COMBINED: >95% Detection Accuracy


SCORING SYSTEM:
───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

Each layer gives SCORE (0-100):

Knox blown: +50 points
Bootloader unlocked: +50 points
Wrong WiFi: +20 points
GPS too perfect: +15 points
No photo: +20 points

THRESHOLD:
├── Score < 30: Accept (low risk)
├── Score 30-60: Warning (medium risk, require additional verification)
├── Score > 60: Block (high risk)
